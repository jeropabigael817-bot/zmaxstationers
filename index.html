Our Mission & About Us

Registered Stationery Dealer and Printing Supplier across East Africa.

ZMAX General Stationers Ltd is a premier, single-stop hub based in Kericho, Kenya. We deal extensively in high-quality educational materials, corporate stationery sets, printing inks, and desktop equipment.

By keeping tight alliances with standard international brands (Canon, Epson, Casio, Oxford), ZMAX guarantees authentic high-durability products. Our business model bridges the gap between major importers and retail-consumers by serving standard units as well as bulk institutional supplies at unmatched retail margins.

Our Vision:

To build the most accessible, high-integrity educational and office logistics stationers across East Africa.

Our Core Promise:

Absolute item genuineness, prompt countrywide deliveries, and robust customer support lines.
